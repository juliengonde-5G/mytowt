About {{ vessel.name }}

{{ vessel.name }} is part of the TOWT fleet of modern cargo sailing vessels, designed to transport goods across the ocean using wind power while offering a unique experience to a limited number of passengers.

Key features

  • Modern sailing cargo vessel optimized for transoceanic routes
